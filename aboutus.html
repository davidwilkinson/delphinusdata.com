About us

Delphinus Data was founded in 2017 by lead AI consultant Dr. David Wilkinson, to offer cutting-edge data science and AI consultancy services to organisations looking to harness the power of artificial intelligence and data science. The typical client of Delphinus is a business that wants to take advantage of the power of data science or build agentic AI systems but needs expert guidance to spearhead technical initiatives, gain organisational buy-in, or build and train AI-focused teams.

David has over 10 years of experience working in AI and data science in a professional capacity, with particular expertise in machine learning and agentic AI. He will be the main point of contact for Delphinus' clients and will often be the principal consultant for client projects.

David has worked on numerous technical projects across many sectors including credit scoring, email outreach models, polling analysis and segmentation, AI agent development, multi-agent architectures, and building technical teams in various organisations. His experience spans from traditional machine learning and data science projects to cutting-edge applications involving generative models and intelligent agent systems. David has also served as Chief Data Officer for a number of start-up technology companies. In these roles, he managed teams that built advanced software products for data strategy, marketing, and politics, which were successfully adopted by many medium and large company clients.
